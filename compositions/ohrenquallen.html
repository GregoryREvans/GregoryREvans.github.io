Ohrenquallen / Aurelia Aurita

score





The following inscription is found at the head of the score:
Generally from 25-40 centimeters in diameter, Ohrenquallen, also known as Aurelia Aurita or the Moon Jellyfish, are recognized by their translucent bodies and horseshoe shaped gonads. Aurelia tend to swim in very large smacks. These medusae are also capable of life-cycle reversal, growing younger rather than older.

Ohrenquallen was written for violinist Jacob Duber.
